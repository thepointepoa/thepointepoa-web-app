About The Pointe at Northshore

The Pointe at Northshore was established in 2000 and is located around 15 miles southwest of Cedar Park, TX. Here you will find The Pointe at Northshore quietly nestled on the picturesque shores of Lake Travis in the beautiful Texas hill country. Please feel free to contact us below with any questions or to request more information.
